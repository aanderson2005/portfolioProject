My Inspiration

Daniel Martinez

Daniel Martinez is the San Francisco Giants Social Designer. His graphics inspire me to create my own. I love the colors and typography he uses. It blends with the Giants brand extremely well.

SZA

SZA has been one of my top artists for the past several years. SZA is a Grammy award winning R&B artist. I can listen to her music at any time, which helps her stand out compared to other artists I listen to.

Shigeru Miyamoto

When I think of Nintendo, I think of Miyamoto. From Mario, to Zelda, his characters and games have impacted millions of gamers. His creativity in the gaming industry is a big reason I fell in love with video games.
